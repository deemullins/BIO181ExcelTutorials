Overview

You will be using Microsoft Excel to execute calculations for various assignments, and we have provided a complete list of tutorials below, for you to access and refer back to at any time throughout the semester.

Note: Install the desktop version of Microsoft Excel on your computer to complete your assignments. The online version of Excel looks and functions differently.

Important! Before your lab, take these steps:

  • Check each module for the tutorials required to complete that week's lab assignment.
  • Download the Scientific Thinking Excel Tutorial Workbook
  • Take notes and complete the Excel worksheet(s) to help you work through your lab assignments.
